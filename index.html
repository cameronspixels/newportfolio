Technically

I'm well tuned to the front-end, enjoying working largely with HTML and CSS. I don't like Javascript very much, and can hack together enough PHP for websites to be - in some way - functional.

Personally

I like to make websites and resources that are useful or mean something to people. There's probably enough of the rest. I'll never ask to send you notifications, I promise.
